# FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about PLUS HUB

## General Questions

### What is PLUS HUB?

PLUS HUB is the largest collection of high rated stuff on the internet! We provide a comprehensive directory of free resources including movies, TV shows, anime, music, games, books, software, and more.

### Is PLUS HUB free to use?

Yes, PLUS HUB is completely free to use. We curate and organize free resources available on the internet.

### Does PLUS HUB host any files?

No, this site does not host any files. We only provide links and information about free resources available elsewhere on the internet.

## Safety & Privacy

### Is it safe to use the resources listed here?

We recommend always using an ad-blocker and VPN when accessing free media. Check our [Ad-block / Privacy](/adblock-privacy) section for recommended security tools.

### What security measures should I take?

- Always use a reliable VPN
- Install and use an ad-blocker (like uBlock Origin)
- Keep your antivirus software updated
- Verify sources before downloading
- Use HTTPS connections when possible

### Is the content legal?

PLUS HUB does not host any files. We provide information and links to resources. Users are responsible for ensuring they comply with local laws and regulations regarding content access and downloading.

### What should I know about copyright?

Always respect copyright laws in your jurisdiction. Support content creators when possible by purchasing official releases.
