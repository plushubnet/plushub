# Feedback

We value your feedback and suggestions! Help us make PLUS HUB better.

## How to Provide Feedback

### Suggestions & Feature Requests

Have an idea to improve PLUS HUB? We'd love to hear it!

- New categories or resources
- UI/UX improvements
- Feature requests
- Content suggestions

### Report Issues

Found a problem? Let us know:

- Broken links
- Missing resources
- Site errors
- Content issues

### General Feedback

Share your thoughts:

- What you like about PLUS HUB
- What could be improved
- Your experience using the platform

## Ways to Submit Feedback

### Community Channels

- **Discord**: Join our Discord community
- **GitHub**: Open an issue on our GitHub repository
- **Reddit**: Share on our subreddit

### Direct Contact

For direct feedback, you can reach out through our community channels listed in the navigation.

## What Happens to Your Feedback?

- All feedback is reviewed by our team
- Suggestions are considered for future updates
- Issues are prioritized and addressed
- We appreciate every contribution!

## Contributing Content

Want to contribute more than feedback? Check out our [Contribute](/contribute) page to learn how you can help add resources and improve PLUS HUB.
