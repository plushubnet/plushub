# Ad-block / Privacy

Learn how to block ads, trackers and other nasty things.

## Ad Blockers

### Browser Extensions

- **uBlock Origin** - Efficient, open-source ad blocking
- **AdGuard** - Advanced ad blocking and privacy protection
- **Privacy Badger** - Blocks invisible trackers

### System-Wide Solutions

- **Pi-hole** - Network-wide ad blocking
- **AdGuard Home** - Network-wide blocker

### VPN Services

- **ProtonVPN** - Free VPN with strong privacy
- **Windscribe** - Generous free tier
- **Mullvad** - Privacy-focused VPN

### Privacy Browsers

- **Brave Browser** - Built-in privacy features
- **Firefox** - Privacy-focused browser
- **Tor Browser** - Maximum anonymity

### Privacy Extensions

- **HTTPS Everywhere** - Force secure connections
- **Decentraleyes** - Block CDN tracking
- **Cookie AutoDelete** - Automatic cookie management
